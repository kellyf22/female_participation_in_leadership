Why is Having Female Participation in Leadership Important?

We have all experienced the value of positive female leadership in our lives. This is something that shapes how we interact with the world and define ourselves. However, females are often left out of important conversations. We wanted to look at the value of female inclusion in leadership positions and what that might look like for the modern workforce.

Call to action: Before women can lead, they have to be involved. This is why equal pay and representation matters at all levels of the workforce.
